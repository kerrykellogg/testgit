About Me

Born in Germany, grew up in NorCal, live in West Village. Music major at Washington and Lee, MBA from Wharton, struggling GA student. Avid water polo player, a mediocre triathlete, and a terrible singer. I hate being still, not finishing a crossword puzzle and treadmills. I love live music, cheap beer and public transportation.

I decided to take this GA class because of a random quote I read about one girl's decision to leave finance to start coding: "Why did I want to do something different? In part, because I wanted something that felt more tangible. But mostly because the story of the internet continues to be the story of our time. I’m pretty sure that if you truly want to follow — or, better still, bend — that story’s arc, you should know how to write code."
